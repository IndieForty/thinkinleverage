**Binance** is the world’s largest crypto trading venue, commanding a market ecosystem far bigger than most conventional exchanges. Its cost of leadership upheaval overshadows many firms—yet it silently shifted again in late 2025.

**Binance Holdings** appointed co-founder **He Yi** as co-CEO alongside **Richard Teng** in December 2025, the most significant top-level change since **Zhao Changpeng** stepped down in 2023. **Zhao** resigned after a guilty plea for anti-money-laundering violations but was later pardoned by US President Donald Trump.

But **Binance's** dual-CEO structure is less about executive ego and more about a strategic layering of control and trust inside a chaotic regulatory environment. This is a shift toward **structural leverage through leadership pairing**.

**“Effective control in turbulent markets requires dual-aligned anchors,”** one crypto analyst summarized.

Industry watchers often see **leadership changes** as simple succession mechanics or damage control after compliance issues. They assume a single CEO rule works best for clarity. That’s wrong—it ignores **regulatory instability as the true constraint** for crypto.

Crypto exchanges like **Coinbase** and **Kraken** have faced costly setbacks due to regulatory scrutiny. But **Binance** navigates the landscape by deeply embedding trusted insiders. ## Dual-CEOs: Creating Redundancy to Mitigate Regulatory Volatility

**He Yi’s** appointment alongside **Richard Teng** adds a governance buffer that actively redistributes executive risk. This dual leadership acts like a built-in compliance circuit breaker, avoiding the single point of failure seen in previous top-heavy models.

Competitors like **Coinbase** operate under more traditional CEO structures, which has led to sharper stock price sensitivities post-legal challenges. **Binance** instead draws on **internal legacy relationships**—He Yi is both co-founder and intimately connected to Zhao Changpeng through family ties, reinforcing trust networks capable of autonomous problem solving without outside interference.
