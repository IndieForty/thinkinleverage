## Why Bundling Multiple Models Beats Specialized Alternatives

Alternative approaches often target best-in-class single AI functionalities—for instance, specialized image generators or code assistants. However, this creates friction for operators who must stitch together outputs manually.

OpenAI’s approach leverages the synergies across models to automate transitions. For instance, content created in the text model can automatically feed into image generation prompts without manual re-entry. This design creates a continuous workflow rather than isolated steps.

Unlike typical SaaS stacks that stack apps via integrations, this platform embeds multiple AI capabilities natively. This structure unlocks automation that works without constant human intervention, a leap beyond simply automating repetitive tasks.
